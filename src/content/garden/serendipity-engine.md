---
title: "The Serendipity Engine"
description: "The real payoff of a linked garden: rediscovering connections you forgot you could make."
date: "2026-06-10"
tags: ["thinking", "pkm"]
---

A pile of notes is inert. The value appears when two notes you wrote months apart turn out to
belong together. [[Backlinks]] surface the connections you *did* make; the graph's latent-link
suggestions surface the ones you *almost* made — pairs with shared neighbors or tags that aren't
linked yet.

This is why [[Nonlinear Thinking]] likes a graph: it manufactures the collisions. [[Maps of Content]]
then capture the collisions worth keeping.
