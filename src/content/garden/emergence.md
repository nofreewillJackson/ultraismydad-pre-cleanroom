---
title: "Emergence"
description: "Global structure that no one designed, arising from many local interactions."
date: "2026-06-11"
tags: ["systems", "emergence"]
---

Emergence is when order at the top is produced by rules at the bottom — flocks from birds,
markets from trades, themes from links. Nobody draws the flock; it appears.

A garden's clusters are emergent: each note only "knows" its own links, yet run a local rule
across all of them ([[Simple Rules]]) and communities fall out ([[Self-Organization]]). That's
the same move [[Nonlinear Thinking]] makes — let structure surface instead of imposing it. See
[[Welcome to the Garden]].
