---
title: "Self-Organization"
description: "Systems that sort themselves into structure without a central planner."
date: "2026-06-11"
tags: ["systems", "emergence"]
---

No conductor tells the notes which cluster to join; they sort themselves by who they link to.
That's self-organization — the engine behind [[Emergence]] and the consequence of [[Simple Rules]].

In the garden it's literal: label propagation lets clusters settle, and you read them off the
colored halos. When one stabilizes, a [[Maps of Content]] note can give it a name.
