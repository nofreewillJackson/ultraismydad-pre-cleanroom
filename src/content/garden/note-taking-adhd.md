---
title: "Note-taking for ADHD"
description: "Externalize, don't organize. Capture first; let structure come later."
date: "2026-06-09"
tags: ["adhd", "pkm"]
---

The failure mode is the empty folder hierarchy you have to decide into *before* you've had the
thought. ADHD brains stall there. Better: capture atomically (see [[Atomic Notes]]), link
loosely, and let [[Maps of Content]] grow after the fact.

This fits how [[Nonlinear Thinking]] actually works, and it respects the real constraint —
[[Working Memory]]. The system holds the structure so you don't have to.
