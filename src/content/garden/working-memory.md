---
title: "Working Memory"
description: "The scarce resource. A second brain is mostly a working-memory prosthesis."
date: "2026-06-08"
tags: ["thinking", "adhd"]
---

You can hold only a few things at once. Every connection you must *remember* is a connection
you can't *make*. So the job of a note system is to take held things and put them somewhere
visible — that's the core of [[Note-taking for ADHD]].

When the graph shows a note's neighbors, you're reading relationships instead of reconstructing
them. That's [[Nonlinear Thinking]] with the load taken off.
