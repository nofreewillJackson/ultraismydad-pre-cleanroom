---
title: "Atomic Notes"
description: "One idea per note — the unit that makes linking and reuse possible."
date: "2026-06-07"
tags: ["pkm", "method"]
---

If a note holds one idea, it can be linked from anywhere that idea is relevant, and reused in
contexts you didn't foresee. Big notes can't do that — they're only findable as a whole.

Atomicity is what makes [[Zettelkasten]] and [[Maps of Content]] work, and it's the gentlest
on-ramp for [[Note-taking for ADHD]]: capture one small thing, link it, move on.
