This is a **digital garden**: a small, interlinked set of notes that grow over time. Unlike
the [[On Static Sites|static archive]] elsewhere on the site, notes here link to each other
freely with `[[wikilinks]]`, and every note shows its #backlinks at the bottom.

> [!tip] Start anywhere
> Gardens aren't read top to bottom. Follow a link that looks interesting and wander. The
> [graph](/garden) on the index shows how everything connects.

## What lives here

- [[Markdown Feature Tour]] — a stress-test of every supported syntax.
- [[Wikilinks and Backlinks]] — how the links and the graph work.
- [[On Static Sites]] — a longer-form note, fully transcluded into the tour.
